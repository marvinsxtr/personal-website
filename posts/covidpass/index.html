CovidPass

June 2021 - June 2022 · Marvin Sextro, Sören Busse, Hauke Tönjes

CovidPass offered the ability to add EU Digital Covid Vaccination Certificates as a pass into wallet apps. CovidPass accomplished this without sending personal data to a server and instead only used a hashed representation for the signing step.

After CovidPass was used over 6 million times across the EU and translated into 11 languages, it was discontinued in June 2022 since iOS introduced a native way of adding EU Digital Covid Certificates to the wallet: Add verifiable COVID-19 vaccination information to Apple Wallet and Health.
